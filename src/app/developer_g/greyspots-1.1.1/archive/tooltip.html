Turn printer on.
Press “SETUP/EXIT”.
Use the “+” button to find the “MAXIMUM LENGTH” setting.
Press the “SELECT” button to edit.
Use the “-” and ”+” buttons to set to “7in”.
Save by pressing “SETUP/EXIT” twice.
Turn printer off.
Turn printer on.
Go to “Printers and Scanners” in settings.
Unlock to allow changes.
Click the “+”.
Choose “Zebra Technologies ZTC ZM400-200dpi ZPL”.
Click “Choose a Driver”.
Driver: “Zebra ZPL Label Printer”.
Click Add.
Print.
